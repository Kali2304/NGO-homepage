About InAmigos Foundation

Founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla (Founder & CEO), InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered non-profit organisation committed to creating lasting social impact across India.

Who We Are

Operating from Chhattisgarh, our organisation works through dedicated volunteers and professionals to solve societal challenges and build stronger communities.

Recognitions

Section 8 Registered, CSR-1 Registered, NITI Aayog Registered, 80G & 12A Certified, ISO 9001:2015 Certified.

Our Mission

Empowering communities through education, women empowerment, sustainability, animal welfare, and humanitarian initiatives.

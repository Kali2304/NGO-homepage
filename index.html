About Us

Empowering Communities Across India

InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization dedicated to improving lives through education, women empowerment, environmental sustainability, animal welfare, and humanitarian support.

Since its establishment, the organization has actively worked across multiple states in India through impactful campaigns, volunteer-driven initiatives, and community outreach programs.

CSR-1 Registered
NITI Aayog Registered
ISO 9001:2015 Certified

Creating meaningful impact through community-driven initiatives

InAmigos Foundation works across multiple sectors to empower communities, improve livelihoods, and create sustainable social change throughout India.

Project SEVA

Supporting underprivileged communities through food distribution, relief drives, clothing donations, and humanitarian outreach programs.

Project BACHPANSHALA

Empowering children through education support, digital literacy, mentorship programs, and learning opportunities.

Project UDAAN

Helping women become financially independent through skill development, training, and entrepreneurship initiatives.

Project PRAKRITI

Promoting environmental sustainability through plantation drives, awareness campaigns, and conservation initiatives.
